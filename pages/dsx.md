# Demonstration and Science Experiments (DSX)

The Demonstration and Science Experiments (DSX) mission was a U.S. Air Force Research Laboratory satellite mission that studied the medium Earth orbit (MEO) environment, particularly the radiation belts.

DSX data has contributed to the development and validation of IRENE-AE9/AP9/SPM models.
