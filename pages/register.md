# Create an Account

To download IRENE-AE9/AP9/SPM, please register for a free public account. Registration allows the AE9/AP9/SPM team to notify you of new releases and bug fixes.

**To register**, send an email to [AFRL.RVBXR.AE9.AP9.Org.Mbx@us.af.mil](mailto:AFRL.RVBXR.AE9.AP9.Org.Mbx@us.af.mil?subject=AE9AP9%20Account%20Request) with:

- Full name
- Organization / affiliation
- Country
- Intended use (brief description)

You will receive a reply with download instructions. We do not share your contact information with third parties.

> *Note: Requests from certain domains may not be fulfilled per U.S. export and distribution policy.*
