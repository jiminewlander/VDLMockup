# AE9/AP9/SPM Team

The IRENE-AE9/AP9/SPM model is developed and maintained by a team at the Air Force Research Laboratory (AFRL), Space Vehicles Directorate, in collaboration with Atmospheric and Environmental Research, Inc. (AER) and The Aerospace Corporation.
